# Dessa Vael

> [!info] At a Glance
> A hauler in her thirties who is not angry so much as she is done pretending the math works.

## Appearance

Lean and sun-dark, with the kind of posture that comes from years of loading and unloading. Her hair is usually half-escaped from a braid. She has a scar across her left palm from a dropped frame pin. She talks with her hands.

## Personality

**First impression:** Practical, a little guarded. She answers questions with other questions until she figures out who she is talking to.

**Deeper impression:** She is very good at counting things. Hours, loads, coins, the gap between what she hauls and what she clears after the tithe. She has done the math on the Calt arrangement and she does not like what it adds up to.

**What they hide:** She is more scared than she lets on. The talk about a haul stoppage was half serious, half venting, and it has spread further than she intended. Now people are looking at her like she has a plan.

## Backstory

Dessa came to [[Caltworks]] three years ago with a partner and a secondhand cart. The work was there and the pay was better than the mill town they left. The Calt tithe was not nothing, but it was workable.

The problem is it has not stayed workable. More haulers arrived, the tithe did not go down, and the Calts are harder to talk to than they were when fewer people were watching. Her partner went back after year two. Dessa stayed because she has a decent reputation with [[Maren Seedeck]]'s weighing house and leaving means starting over.

She started talking to other haulers about the stoppage idea because she needed someone else to confirm she was not imagining the situation. Now she is the nearest thing to an organizer that the haulers have, without having sought that.

## What They Want

**Conscious goal:** A reduction in the tithe, or at minimum, a formal accounting of what it is for and where it goes.

**Deeper need:** She wants the work to be worth the work. She would stop organizing tomorrow if the numbers made sense.

> [!warning]- GM Only
> **Campaign role:** The pressure valve. If the players want to understand the tension in [[Caltworks]], she will tell them plainly. If they want to intervene in the situation, she is the lever.
>
> **Key secret:** She has been offered money by [[Vess Cordham]] (through an intermediary, so she does not yet know his name) to help the stoppage happen at a specific time, which happens to coincide with a large Calt shipment. She has not taken it. She has not said no yet. Cordham's interest is in disrupting a Calt shipment to [[Rackby]] at a moment that benefits his supplier transition.
>
> **Arc trajectory:** If the stoppage happens, it either works (major power shift, she becomes a significant figure) or it does not (she leaves or is made an example). If the outside offer surfaces, the other haulers will wonder what her actual motivations are.
>
> **Roleplay notes:**
> - Speech pattern: Plain and direct. She uses numbers as evidence. No dramatic declarations.
> - Mannerisms: Keeps her hands moving, wrapping and unwrapping a piece of leather cord she carries.
> - Sample dialogue: "I did the hauls, I paid the tithe, I checked the ledger. The percentage went up. That is the whole conversation."
